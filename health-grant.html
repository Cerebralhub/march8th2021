Health Reward Grant (N1,000,000) - 2021

Health Reward Grant - N1,000,000



This year, the March 8th Initiative expanded in terms of reach and scope. The March 8th Initiative recognises health workers nationwide who, in an extremely difficult time, continue to exhibit compassion, diligence, hard work and professionalism in carrying out their duties. Individuals were requested to nominate any health worker who has shown these qualities during the past year under a Health Grant Reward Category that acknowledges their invaluable service to our communities.

The nominations were to be filled out on the march8th.ng portal providing information about their nominee and the reason(s) for the nomination.

Entries opened at 12.00 am on Monday, the 8th of March, 2021 and closed at 11:59pm on Sunday, the 14th of March, 2021. A total of 1,520 nominations were received.






SCREENING

The nominations were evaluated for clarity and contribution of the nominee to the development of the health sector in the past year. The impact of the nominee on the society was also evaluated.


VERIFICATION AND SELECTION

The applicants who qualified for the Final Screening were contacted to submit academic credentials, professional licences and other supporting documents. They also provided details of their work supervisors for verification.

At the end of the verification process, thirty-four Applicants from across Nigeria were shortlisted as winners of the 2021 Health Reward Grant.
