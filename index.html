About March 8th Initiative


The March 8th Initiative, a brainchild of the “Friends of Prof” is organised to inspire and promote communal, entrepreneurial and public-spirited endeavours within Nigeria in honour of His Excellency, Prof. Yemi Osinbajo, SAN..

2022 Application

Youths will submit their Proposals on the resources, skills and opportunities to harness same in their community on the portal.

Application Instruction

Please read the below instructions below to avoid disqualification

  • Submit documents (pdf or word) only.
  • The platform collects your email to submit
  • Your proposal should not be more than 1000words.
